How it works

A simple courier marketplace between customers and vehicle owners.

01

Customer requests

The customer chooses pickup, destination, vehicle size, load type, and any handling needs.

02

Platform prices

The system estimates distance and calculates a transparent price before confirmation.

03

Driver accepts

A nearby driver receives the trip, accepts it, and proceeds to the pickup point.

04

Trip tracked

Both parties receive status updates from pickup to delivery completion.
